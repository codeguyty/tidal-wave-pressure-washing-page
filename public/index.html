House Washing

Power washing is a highly efficient technique that can safely be used to clean mold, dust, rust, mud and other dirt from a wide variety of surfaces. It is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve the look and value of your home.

Our low-to-high pressure cleaning systems have numerous residential applications and are most commonly used to clean siding, roofs, gutters, driveways and decks. In addition, we offer a full array of commercial pressure washing services on materials ranging from wood to concrete.

So whether you want to remove graffiti, oil and grease, mildew, or any other unwanted stains, we have the equipment and expertise to provide you with a complete customized cleaning solution.

Window Cleaning

Most people don’t pay too much attention to their windows, and cleaning is usually overlooked. But clean and streak-free windows are more important than you might think. Windows are important for a multitude of reasons, and the main reason is to allow enough light inside your home or business. However, if there are stains, dirt, and fingerprints on them, much less light will enter your room. And it has been scientifically proven that sunlight has a positive effect on how people feel. Dirty windows can also affect the curb appeal of your home.

That’s why Tidal Wave Power Washing offers professional services that will make your windows completely shine again. Windows are among the most essential parts of your home or business place, and they are a reflection of your hygiene and business success. Scientists have proven that people get a first impression in just a few seconds and ask themselves what they first see when they pull into someone’s home or place of business? It is the building or house, its cleanliness, and its appearance.

Our window washing services will make sure that your windows are immaculately clean on the outside so that everyone can enjoy the view and clean windows.
